### Executive Summary

Total revenue reached $7.7M. We processed 5,000 orders. The average order value was $1,540. Sales are trending down. We must address this decline. Customer segmentation shows opportunities. We can improve customer retention.

*   Total Revenue: $7.7M. This is our total sales.
*   Total Orders: 5,000. This is the number of orders placed.
*   Average Order Value: $1,540. Each order averaged this amount.
*   Total Units Sold: 10,577. This is the total items sold.
*   Unique Customers: 994. We had this many individual buyers.
*   Average Revenue Per Customer: $7,747. Each customer spent this much.
*   Total Products Sold: 20. We offer this many different items.

### Key Drivers & Trends

*   **Declining Monthly Sales:** Sales fell from January to November. January sales were $2.1M. November sales were only $87.8K.
*   **Negative Growth:** Average monthly growth was -13.72%. This shows a significant drop.
*   **Product Category Performance:** Electronics led with $3.7M in sales. Furniture followed with $2.3M.
*   **Customer Segmentation:** "Champions" spent the most. "Lost Customers" spent the least.
*   **Regional Performance:** Latin America performed best. They generated $1.6M in sales.

### Recommendations

*   **Address Sales Decline:** Investigate the cause of the decline. Implement strategies to boost sales.
*   **Focus on High-Value Customers:** Increase engagement with "Champions." Encourage repeat purchases.
*   **Optimize Product Portfolio:** Promote top-selling products. Consider phasing out underperforming items.
*   **Regional Focus:** Analyze Latin America's success. Apply those strategies to other regions.
