3. Product Category Performance: Electronics led all categories. They generated $3.7M in sales.

1. Address Declining Sales: Investigate the root causes of the decline. How to implement: Conduct market research and customer surveys.
2. Focus on High-Value Customers: Increase engagement with "Champions". How to implement: Personalized marketing campaigns and loyalty programs.
3. Optimize Product Portfolio: Promote top-selling products. How to implement: Bundle deals and targeted advertising for electronics.
