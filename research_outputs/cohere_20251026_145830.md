### Key Insights and Recommendations

**1. Reverse Sales Decline:**
- Our top priority is reversing the sales decline.
- Focus on retaining at-risk customers and winning back lost customers.
- Consider targeted campaigns and personalized offers.

**2. Optimize Product Mix:**
- Electronics and furniture are our top-selling categories.
- Optimize the product portfolio to drive sales in these categories.
- Consider promotions or partnerships to boost sales.

**3. Regional Focus:**
- Latin America is our top-performing region.
- Analyze regional trends to replicate success in other regions.
- Tailor marketing and product offerings to regional preferences.

**4. Customer Retention:**
- At-risk and lost customers represent a significant portion of our base.
- Implement proactive retention strategies, like loyalty programs or personalized support.
- Targeted campaigns can re-engage and win back customers.

**5. Anomaly Analysis:**
- Investigate sales anomalies to understand causes.
- Use insights to optimize pricing, promotions, and inventory management.
- Leverage spikes to drive more consistent sales.

In summary, we must address the sales decline while optimizing our product mix and customer retention. By focusing on these areas, we can improve performance and drive sustainable growth.
