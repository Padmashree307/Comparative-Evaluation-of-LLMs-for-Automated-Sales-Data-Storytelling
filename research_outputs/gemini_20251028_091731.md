### Executive Summary

Total revenue reached $7.7M. We processed 5,000 orders. The average order value was $1,540. Sales are declining. The average monthly decline is 13.72%. We must address this critical trend. Customer segmentation shows opportunities. We have many loyal customers.

### Financial Performance

*   Total Revenue: $7.7M. This means we earned 7.7 million dollars.
*   Total Orders: 5,000. We fulfilled five thousand orders.
*   Average Order Value: $1,540. Each order averaged $1,540.
*   Total Units Sold: 10,577. We sold ten thousand units.
*   Unique Customers: 994. Almost one thousand customers bought.
*   Average Revenue Per Customer: $7,747. Each customer spent $7,747.
*   Total Products Sold: 20. We offer twenty different products.

### Key Drivers & Trends

*   **Declining Monthly Sales:** Sales decreased each month. January sales were $2.1M. November sales dropped to $87.8K.
*   **Negative Growth:** Average monthly growth is -13.72%. This is a significant downward trend.
*   **Product Category Performance:** Electronics lead with $3.7M in sales. Furniture follows with $2.3M.
*   **Customer Segmentation:** Champions are our best customers. Loyal customers are also valuable. "At Risk" customers need attention.

### Recommendations

*   **Address Sales Decline:** Investigate the cause of the decline. Implement strategies to boost sales.
*   **Focus on Top Products:** Promote top-selling products. Smartphone X, Smartwatch, and Laptop Pro are key.
*   **Retain "At Risk" Customers:** Create targeted campaigns. Offer incentives to retain these customers.
*   **Optimize Marketing Spend:** Analyze regional performance. Focus on high-performing regions like Latin America.
