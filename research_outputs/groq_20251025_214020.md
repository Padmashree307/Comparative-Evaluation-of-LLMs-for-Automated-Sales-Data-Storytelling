1. **Optimize Product Portfolio**: Invest in high-demand categories such as Electronics and Software, and discontinue underperforming products. Owner: Product Manager. Metric: Increase sales revenue by 10% within 90 days.
2. **Enhance Customer Retention**: Develop and implement targeted marketing campaigns to retain high-value customers and minimize churn. Owner: Marketing Manager. Metric: Reduce churn rate by 5% within 60 days.

4. **Improve Operational Efficiency**: Streamline our sales processes to increase average order value and reduce operational costs. Owner: Sales Operations Manager. Metric: Increase average order value by 5% within 60 days.
5. **Develop Customer Lifetime Value Strategy**: Create a customer lifetime value strategy to maximize revenue from our high-value customers. Owner: Customer Success Manager. Metric: Increase customer lifetime value by 10% within 90 days.
6. **Conduct Market Research**: Conduct market research to identify emerging trends and opportunities in the B2B technology/SaaS industry. Owner: Market Research Analyst. Metric: Identify 2 new market opportunities within 30 days.
