### Recommendations

- **Address Sales Decline:** Identify causes of the downward trend. Implement strategies to reverse the decline, focusing on high-value products and regions.
- **Retain High-Value Customers:** Develop targeted strategies for Champions and Loyal Customers. Encourage repeat purchases and increase loyalty.
- **At-Risk Customer Strategy:** Implement re-engagement campaigns for At-Risk customers. Offer incentives to prevent further decline.
- **Product Portfolio Review:** Optimize the product mix. Consider new products or categories to sustain growth.
- **Regional Focus:** Leverage the success in Latin America. Explore expansion or targeted marketing in this region.
