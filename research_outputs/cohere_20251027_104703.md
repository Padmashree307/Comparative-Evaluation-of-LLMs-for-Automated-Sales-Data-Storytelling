### Executive Summary

Our total revenue for the year is $7,701,204, with 5,000 orders and an average order value of $1,540. This report highlights three critical areas: overall business performance, a declining sales trend, and customer value distribution.

### Overall Business Performance
- **Total Revenue:** $7,701,204
- **Total Orders:** 5,000
- **Average Order Value:** $1,540
- **Total Units Sold:** 10,577

### Declining Sales Trend
Monthly sales show a downward trend, with an average monthly decline of 13.72%. The best month was January 2024, with $2,100,327 in sales, and the worst was November, with $87,816.

### Strategic Recommendations

To address the declining sales trend and optimize our product portfolio:
1. **Focus on High-Value Customers:** Develop strategies to retain and grow this segment, as they significantly impact revenue.
2. **At-Risk Customer Retention:** Implement targeted campaigns to re-engage at-risk customers and prevent further decline.
3. **Product Line Review:** Assess the electronics and furniture categories, which account for over 75% of sales, to identify opportunities for new products or improvements.
4. **Regional Strategies:** Tailor marketing and sales efforts to each region, leveraging the insights from the top-performing Latin America region.

This report provides a comprehensive overview of our sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends. By addressing the critical areas outlined, we can develop effective strategies to improve our sales and customer engagement.
