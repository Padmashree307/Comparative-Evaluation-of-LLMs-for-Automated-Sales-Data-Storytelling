### Executive Summary

Our total revenue for the year is $7.7M, with 5,000 orders and an average order value of $1,540. This performance is concerning due to a downward sales trend. We must focus on reversing this trend and improving customer value.

### Performance Overview

- Total Revenue: $7,701,204 (from 5,000 orders)
- Average Order Value: $1,540.24
- Total Units Sold: 10,577
- Unique Customers: 994
- Average Revenue Per Customer: $7,747.69
- Total Products Sold: 20

### Key Insights

**1. Sales Trend:**
- Monthly sales show a 13.72% average decline.
- The downward trend is strong, with a value of 160,574.77.
- January was the peak, and November the lowest month.

**2. Product Performance:**
- Electronics and Furniture categories dominate, with $3.7M and $2.3M in sales, respectively.
- Top 5 products: Smartphone X, Smartwatch, Laptop Pro, Tablet Plus, and Headphones.

**3. Customer Segmentation:**
- 25.75% are high-value customers, while 18.21% are at risk.
- 'Champions' and 'Loyal Customers' segments have the highest value and frequency.
- 'At Risk' customers have low recency and frequency, indicating potential churn.

**4. Regional Performance:**
- Latin America is the top region, with $1.62M in sales.
- Asia Pacific, Europe, and North America are close behind, each with over $1.5M.

**5. Anomalies:**
- 17 days with unusual sales spikes.
- The highest spike was on December 31, with sales of $127,109.91.

### Recommendations

- **Address Sales Decline:** Identify causes of the downward trend. Implement strategies to boost sales, focusing on high-value products and regions.
- **Customer Retention:** Develop targeted strategies for 'At Risk' customers to improve their engagement and reduce churn.
- **Product Focus:** Optimize the product portfolio. Consider new offerings in the top-performing categories to maintain growth.
- **Regional Strategy:** Leverage the strong performance in Latin America. Explore ways to replicate this success in other regions.
- **Anomaly Analysis:** Investigate the unusual sales spikes. Understand the drivers to capitalize on these opportunities.
