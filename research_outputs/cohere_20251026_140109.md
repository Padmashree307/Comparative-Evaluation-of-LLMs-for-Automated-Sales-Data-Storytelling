4. **Re-engage At-Risk Customers:** Develop strategies to re-engage the 18.21% of customers at risk of churn. Personalized outreach and incentives can help.

In summary, while our overall revenue is strong, the declining sales trend is a critical issue. We must act swiftly to understand and reverse this trend, focusing on product optimization, customer retention, and regional performance.
