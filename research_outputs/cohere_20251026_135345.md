### Executive Summary

- Total revenue exceeded $7.7M, but sales declined.
- Average order value was $1,540.24.
- Key issues: declining sales trend and customer value distribution.

### Financial Performance

- Total Revenue: $7,701,204.28 (down from peak sales of $2,100,327.1 in January).
- Total Orders: 5,000.
- Average Order Value: $1,540.24.
- Unique Customers: 994.
- Average Revenue per Customer: $7,747.69.

### Key Drivers & Trends

1. Sales Trend: Monthly sales showed a downward trend, with an average monthly decline of -13.72%. This means sales decreased by 13.72% each month.
2. Product Performance: Electronics and furniture categories dominated, with $3.7M and $2.3M in sales, respectively.
3. Customer Segmentation: 'Champions' and 'Loyal Customers' contributed significantly. 'At Risk' customers (18.21%) need attention.
4. Regional Performance: Latin America led with $1.62M in sales, followed by Asia-Pacific and Europe at $1.57M each.

### Recommendations

1. Sales Strategy: Diversify product portfolio beyond electronics and furniture. Focus on categories with growth potential.
2. Customer Retention: Implement targeted strategies for 'At Risk' customers. Use incentives to encourage repeat purchases.
3. Regional Focus: Invest in Latin America to maintain momentum. Explore new markets in this region.
4. Sales Team Training: Educate the team on customer segmentation. Prioritize high-value segments.

How to Implement:
- Product Portfolio: Conduct market research. Identify new products to complement existing offerings.
- Customer Retention: Use CRM data to identify 'At Risk' customers. Offer personalized discounts or loyalty programs.
- Regional Expansion: Hire a regional sales manager for Latin America. Research local market trends and competitors.
- Sales Training: Organize workshops on customer segmentation. Provide tools for tracking and managing customer segments.
