# Q4 Sales Report: Sales Down 14%

## 1. KEY METRICS
- Total Revenue: $7.7 million, a 14% drop from the initial $21 million.
- Total Orders: 5,000, a 75% decrease from 20,000.
- Average Order Value: $1,540, a 2% rise.
- Unique Customers: 994, a 1% increase.
- Average Revenue Per Customer: $7,748.

## 2. WHAT HAPPENED
- There was a decline in sales, with a 13.72% average monthly drop.
- January started strong at $2.1 million, but December ended low at $181,047.
- Sales peaked at $2.1 million in January and fell to $87,816 in November.
- The average sale in electronics was $2,956, and 1,253 orders were placed.
- Furniture sales averaged $1,862 per sale, with 1,237 orders.
- Office supplies had 1,290 orders, with an average sale of $219.60.
- The average sale in software was $1,157, with 1,220 orders.

## 3. WHY IT MATTERS
The sales decline is a concern, especially with a downward trend. We need to address the drop in total orders and average order value.

## 4. WHAT TO DO
- Focus on retaining loyal and champion customers.
- Develop strategies to bring back 'At Risk' customers.
- Enhance product offerings in electronics and software to boost sales.
- Analyze the reasons behind the sales decline and take action.
