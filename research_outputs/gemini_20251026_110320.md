## Sales are down. We must act quickly to reverse this.

**Key Metrics:**

*   Total revenue was $7.7M.
*   We had 5,000 total orders.
*   Average order value was $1,540.24.
*   We acquired 994 unique customers.
*   Average revenue per customer was $7,747.69.

**What Happened:**

*   Sales are trending down.
*   Our average monthly growth is -13.72%.
*   January 2024 was our best month. Sales were $2.1M.
*   November 2024 was our worst month. Sales were $87.8K.
*   Electronics sales were $3.7M. Furniture sales were $2.3M.
*   "Champions" are our best customers. They spend $13.3K on average.
*   "Lost Customers" spent $1.8K on average.

**Why It Matters:**

*   Declining sales hurt our revenue.
*   We risk missing our yearly goals.
*   Losing customers impacts future growth.
*   We need to retain valuable customers.

**What To Do:**

1.  Investigate the cause of the sales decline immediately.
2.  Create a customer retention program. Target "At Risk" customers.
3.  Focus on selling more electronics and furniture.
