# Sales are down. We must act now to reverse this trend.

**Key Metrics:**

*   Total revenue was $7.7M.
*   We had 5,000 total orders.
*   The average order was $1,540.24.
*   Our monthly sales fell 13.72%.
*   We have 994 unique customers.

**What Happened:**

*   Sales peaked in January at $2.1M.
*   Sales hit a low in November at $87.8K.
*   Electronics sales were $3.7M.
*   Furniture sales reached $2.3M.
*   Office supplies sales were only $283K.
*   "At Risk" customers are 18.21% of total.
*   Champions are 25.75% of our customers.

**Why It Matters:**

*   Falling sales will hurt profits.
*   We risk losing more customers.
*   We must boost customer loyalty.
*   We need to find out why sales are down.
*   We must act fast to get back on track.

**What To Do:**

1.  Investigate the sales decline immediately.
2.  Offer special deals to "At Risk" customers.
3.  Focus on top selling products.
4.  Improve customer retention programs.
