### Recommendations

- **Address Sales Decline:** Identify causes. Implement strategies to reverse the trend.
- **Product Focus:** Optimize electronics and furniture offerings.

- **Anomaly Analysis:** Investigate unusual sales days. Adjust strategies for consistent performance.
