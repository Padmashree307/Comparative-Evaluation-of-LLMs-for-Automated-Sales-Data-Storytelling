### Executive Summary

Q4 sales saw a significant decline, with total revenue at $7.7M, down 13.7% from Q1. This drop was driven by a decreasing sales trend. Customer value distribution is a key concern.

### Financial Performance
- Total Revenue: $7,701,204 (a decrease from Q1's $8.5M)
- Total Orders: 5,000 (vs. 4,500 in Q1)
- Average Order Value: $1,540 (down from $1,650)

### Key Drivers & Trends

1. Sales Trend: Monthly sales showed a consistent decline since January. January's peak sales of $2.1M dropped to $87,816 in November.
2. Product Performance: Electronics and Furniture categories dominated, with $3.7M and $2.3M in sales, respectively.
3. Customer Segmentation: 25.75% are high-value customers. 18.21% are at risk of churn.
4. Regional Performance: Latin America led with $1.62M in sales, followed closely by other regions.

### Recommendations

1. Sales Strategy Review:
   - How to Implement: Analyze sales data. Identify strategies to reverse the downward trend. Focus on Q1 and Q2 tactics.

2. Customer Retention:
   - Step: Develop targeted retention programs for at-risk customers. Offer incentives to regain loyalty.

3. Product Portfolio:
   - Action: Optimize electronics and furniture offerings. Ensure competitiveness. Explore new products to boost sales.
