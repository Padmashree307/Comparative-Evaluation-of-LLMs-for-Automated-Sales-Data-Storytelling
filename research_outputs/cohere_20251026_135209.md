### Executive Summary

- Total revenue reached $7.7M, with 5,000 orders and an average order value of $1,540.
- A concerning downward sales trend is our main challenge.
- Customer value distribution reveals opportunities with loyal and at-risk groups.

### Financial Performance

- Total Revenue: $7,701,204 (total sales amount in dollars)
- Total Orders: 5,000 (number of orders placed)
- Average Order Value: $1,540.24 (average spending per order)

### Key Drivers & Trends

1. **Declining Sales:** Monthly sales showed a worrying 13.72% average decline, with the highest sales in January 2024 ($2.1M) and the lowest in November 2024 ($87,815).
2. **Product Mix:** Electronics and furniture dominate sales, with $3.7M and $2.3M respectively. Software sales reached $1.4M.
3. **Regional Performance:** Latin America led with $1.62M in sales, followed closely by Europe and North America at $1.57M each.
4. **Customer Segmentation:** 'Champions' and 'Loyal Customers' contribute significantly. 'At Risk' customers (18.21%) need attention.

### Recommendations

1. **Address Sales Decline:**
   - How to implement: Analyze market trends and customer feedback. Adjust pricing and promotions.

2. **Optimize Product Offerings:**
   - How to implement: Review product categories. Phase out low-performing items. Invest in top-selling products like smartphones and laptops.

3. **Focus on Customer Retention:**
   - How to implement: Develop targeted strategies for 'At Risk' customers. Offer incentives to re-engage and improve customer loyalty.

4. **Regional Strategy:**
   - How to implement: Assign dedicated teams for top-performing regions (Latin America, Europe, and North America). Tailor marketing and support to local needs.
